Hobbies

This page is dedicated to my hobbies, some interesting, some not-so interesting, but all of them I pursue and indulge in very much with enjoyment. If you think we can hit it off regarding any of these, don't hesitate to drop me a message to talk more about it!

Aviation and Flight Simulation

I've always been passionate about aviation, ever since I hopped on an Airbus A320 at a very young age. I have cultivated a deep interest in both real-world flying and flight simulation, as flight simulation is as close as you could get to the real thing. With a keen understanding of aviation principles and procedures, I immerse myself in the world of flight through hands-on experiences and virtual simulations.

In the realm of flight simulation, I frequently use popular flight simulation software such as X-Plane and Microsoft Flight Simulator to pass my time but at the same time learn more about flying, procedures, and the world. I recreate realistic flight scenarios, honing my piloting skills and expanding my knowledge of aircraft systems and operations. It's my favorite hobby.

Photography

Seeing airplanes made me capture them, and this is how I got into the hobby of photography. I harness some of my creativity and technical skills to learn how Digital SLR cameras worked, and began to capture moments that resonated with me, or moments I wanted to treasure forever. visually and emotionally. Photography serves as a medium for storytelling and self-expression for me. Meteorology of Tropical Cyclones

My fascination with meteorology of tropical cyclones began when I was studying at Florida Atlantic University in 2019, and that is when we had to evacuate for a potential hit from Hurricane Dorian, a potent Category-5 cyclone that ended up stalling over the Bahamas and causing untold misery and destruction. The sheer power of these tropical entities captivated me to no end, and I've been hooked on their genesis, path, strength and eventual demise ever since.

Tracking tropical cyclones isn't just a hobby for meâ€”it's a passion that drives me to stay informed, analyze data, and anticipate the behavior of these formidable storms. From studying atmospheric conditions and oceanic patterns to monitoring satellite imagery and Doppler radar, I immerse myself in every aspect of tropical cyclone dynamics, seeking to unravel the mysteries of their formation, intensification, and movement. How much would windshear inhibit development? Would the subtropical high steer the cyclone more westward? Will Vortical Hot Towers signal a period of rapid intensification? I love to seek answers to all of these.

Moreover, my passion extends beyond mere observation; it fuels a desire to educate and raise awareness about the importance of preparedness and resilience in vulnerable communities. Through outreach initiatives, social media engagement on Discord, I strive to disseminate accurate information, dispel myths, and empower individuals to make informed decisions in the face of impending tropical cyclones.

Additionally, I actively contribute to online forums and discussions, exchanging insights with fellow meteorology enthusiasts and weather aficionados. Sharing observations, analysis, and forecasting techniques, I foster a collaborative environment where knowledge is freely shared and collective understanding is deepened.

Manga Translating and Typesetting

This one might seem off-beat, but I've been a fan of specific types and genres of manga, and unfortunately found the english versions missing or lacking. I, then, took it upon myself to clean (the Japanese text off the dialouge boxes), translate the Japanese to English, and typeset (basically paste English text on the pages) myself, and eventually I have built a team on Discord that regularly cleans, translates, and typesets manga into English. in the vibrant world of manga, I've discovered a passion for the intricate art of translation and typesetting. Beyond mere enjoyment of the stories and characters, I find immense fulfillment in bridging linguistic and cultural barriers, bringing beloved manga titles to a wider audience through accurate and engaging translations.
