Engineering Projects

I have passionately dedicated my efforts to several projects, investing my time from analyzing nitrous oxide as an oxidizer to conducting steady-state thermal analysis on CubeSat parts, these endeavors exemplify a selection of the most captivating projects I have undertaken as an engineering student.

Hybrid High-Powered Rockets

As a member of the Technology and Aerospace Club's Mechanical Division, I contributed to our achievements in the prestigious amateur rocket competition hosted by NASA. I was tasked with designing, and constructing hybrid rockets capable of excelling in two distinct categories - maximum altitude and precision targeting at 2000 feet.

  • Learn more about the rockets

ESSENCE CubeSat

As a member of the ESSENCE CubeSat project team, spearheaded by York University and in collaboration with the Canadian Space Agency and various other space agencies, I undertook the role of handling the mechanical side of the CubeSat, contributing in design optimization, component integration, and thermal analysis to ensure the successful development and deployment of the satellite into Low Earth Orbit (LEO).

  • Learn more about the ESSENCE CubeSat

Scale Airports for Flight Simulators

As a passionate aviation hobbyist, I embarked on a journey to create highly detailed scale representations of real-world airports for flight simulation programs, enriching the training experiences of real-world pilots. This project not only honed my skills in digital modeling and texture manipulation but also fostered a deep appreciation for aviation, its virtual world-building and how a project is to be handled from start to finish.

  • Learn more about the scale airports

Automatic Plant Watering System

I developed an automatic watering system for plants, leveraging Arduino programming and Java to create intelligent software capable of monitoring soil moisture levels and automatically watering plants when necessary. This project tested my programming skillset to create sustainable gardening practices, resulting in a versatile and efficient solution for plant care.

  • Learn more about the Automatic Plant Watering System
