About Me

Hello, I hope you're having a great day, and welcome to my little website where I showcase my stuff!

My name is Sidhant Vaidya, and I am an extremely passionate and driven individual when it comes to Engineering and my hobbies. I'm currently pursuing a Mechanical Engineering degree. My academics and work experience have enabled me to gain a unique blend of technical and managerial expertise.

As you explore this website I have created, you'll get to take a peak into the projects I have done in the past, the work expereience I hold and, the varied hobbies I take part in. From CAD Design, to programming, and launcing actual mini rockets, I've had the privilege of being involved in a wide array of hands-on experiences, and participate in a wide variety of hobbies that constitute a strong pillar of passion in what I want to pursue. Working within these fast-paced, multi-disciplinary team environments, I've had the wonderful chance to hone my skills in time management, ensuring efficient and successful outcomes.

Beyond my professional pursuits, I also have a passion for photography, everything that flies, geography, going to the gym, biking and creating realistic 3D renditions of airports that can be used in flight training and simulation softwares.

I have lived in multiple cities, on multiple continents and consider myself a global citizen at this point, but based close to the global hub that is New York, I'm always seeking new challenges and opportunities to make a positive impact. Thank you again for having a look at my portfolio, and I hope you enjoy reading about my projects and browsing through my passion projects.
